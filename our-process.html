Our Process

Working with us is easy

From the time you contact us, we’ll get back to you within 24 hours. At that point, we will listen to you – your goals, your needs, your budget – and create a white-board/wire-frame outlining the design and functionality of your application that is within your budget, and submit it to you for your approval. Once we've agreed on the details of the project, we’ll work hard to build your application, and have the project completed on time.

Step 1

1. We listen to your goals, needs and budget

Step 2

2. We white-board every detail of your application so you know exactly what's being built

Step 3

3. Once we've agreed on budget and design, we'll start building your application

Step 4

4. We work tirelessly to build your custom application from the ground up

Step 5

5. We deliver your custom application on time and on budget
