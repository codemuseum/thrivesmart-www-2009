A Little About YouBook

Find the best appointment for you. All online.

What It Is & How it Works

YouBook (by ThriveSmart, Inc.) is a website that allows you to book appointments with participating businesses online. After creating an account and logging in, you can browse available appointment times to book using our intuitive calendar interface. Once you select your favorite appointment time, you simply type in any additional information the business requests, and click the 'Book Appointment' button to confirm. You'll get an email confirmation as well as an email reminder. And if you need to, you can go back and cancel your appointment in your personalized appointment history.

Why Do I Need to Make An Account?

To keep your personal information confidential, YouBook users create accounts where all their personal information is stored. Each appointment you make will be linked to your account and easily accessed whenever you log in.

For Businesses

YouBook is a platform for businesses to accept appointments from their customers online. YouBook is highly customizable to businesses of many types, and easy to set up. To learn more, please see the YouBook product page.
