YouBook Program Policies

February 3, 2007

The goal of the ThriveSmart YouBook program is to help businesses book more appointments by providing a fast, convenient, and safe online reservation experience for their customers. This document outlines the ThriveSmart YouBook policies and guidelines that help ensure customers have a positive interaction with ThriveSmart YouBook businesses.

As a ThriveSmart YouBook business, you're required to adhere to the ThriveSmart YouBook policies at all times. In addition to the policies, we strongly recommend that you follow the ThriveSmart YouBook guidelines (in italics), which are intended to help you generate more repeat reservations by providing your customers with an exceptional user experience.

ThriveSmart reserves the right to expand or edit these policies at any time. ThriveSmart will also exercise its sole discretion in the interpretation and enforcement of these policies in conjunction with the program's Terms of Service.

Customer Information Data Security

  • Do not sell or rent buyer information acquired through ThriveSmart YouBook You may not sell or rent buyer information acquired through ThriveSmart YouBook to third parties at any time.
  • Follow Governmental Security Requirements If you are subject to any governmental security requirements, including but not limited to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), you are required to follow these requirements for any information acquired through ThriveSmart YouBook at all times.
