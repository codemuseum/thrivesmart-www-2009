This has been extracted from years of experience creating successful websites for local businesses, particularly for optometrists. The document itself is a great complement to your Sprout! Website, to guide you along as you fill in the pages of your site. Don't forget to read the bottom, which is possibly the most important part: how to market your website so that people can find it when looking for an optometrist or searching the web.

  4. When Done: Link Building

    The most effective way to get new hits to your website is to ask the following places to add links to your practice's site. We help do this for you with our Mentor and Outsource Packages.
    • Yelp
      Go to your listing and click "Edit Business Info"
    • Google Maps
      Go to your listing and click "Edit" and then click "Claim your Business"
    • Yahoo Local
      Go to your listing and click "Add Info Here"
    • Insider Pages
      Go to your listing and click the blue "Is this your business?" button
    • Yellow Pages
      Go to your listing and click the "Make your listing better" at the bottom left
    • All Vision Insurance Directories
      Most vision insurance companies have a directory of doctors, and can list your site.
    • Local & State Optometry Societies
      Some optometry societies have a list of member doctors, and can add a link to you.
    • Affiliated Practices (e.g. Laser Surgery Clinics)
      Ask practices who refer patients to you to post a link to your site.
    • Buying Groups
      Ask buying groups you participate in to post a link to your site.
    • All About Vision
      Get a link from All About Vision if you put up a link to All About Vision
    • Fellowships & Awards
      Links from any websites of fellowships or awards you have won. Please write them below.
       
    • Alma Mater Alumni Site
      A few (not many, unfortunately) schools will post links to their alumni practices. Please write the optometry school(s) your optometrists graduated from.
       
    • Other Ideas
      Please write them below.
       
    • Google, Yahoo, MSN Search
      Your link will automatically be added to the major search engines.
